Meet Our Founder

The visionary behind Venus Catering's culinary excellence

Shakti Mishra

Founder & Executive Chef

The Culinary Visionary

With over 14 years of experience in the events and catering industry, Shakti Mishra has worked with renowned brands such as Lucky India (Bhubaneswar), Adventure Luxury Hotels, C.K Caterers, Taste of China, Glorious Caterers, Green Chillyz, Kake De Minar, and many more.

Now, as the founder of WOW Caterer, he leads a dedicated team, bringing innovation and excellence to the catering industry. With a passion for creating new and unique recipes, he ensures that every event is a delightful culinary experience.

Professional Journey

Explore the remarkable career path that shaped Shakti Mishra into the culinary expert he is today

Lucky India, Bhubaneswar

2008 - 2010

Started career as a junior chef, mastering the fundamentals of Indian cuisine and catering operations.

Adventure Luxury Hotels

2010 - 2012

Worked as a chef de partie, handling high-volume luxury events and developing expertise in fine dining.

Taste of China & Green Chillyz

2012 - 2015

Expanded culinary repertoire to include Asian cuisines, becoming proficient in fusion cooking techniques.

Glorious Caterers & Kake De Minar

2015 - 2018

Promoted to Executive Chef, overseeing kitchen operations and menu development for large-scale events.

WOW Caterer

2018 - Present

Founded WOW Caterer, bringing together years of expertise to create a premier catering service focused on innovation and excellence.
