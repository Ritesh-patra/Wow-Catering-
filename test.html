Shakti Mishra

With over 20 years of experience in the events and catering industry, Shakti Mishra has worked with renowned MNCs such as Royal Heritage, Adventure Luxury Hotels, C.K Caterers, Taste of China, Glorious Caterers, Green Chillyz, Kake De Minar, and many more. Now, as the founder of WOW Caterer, he leads a dedicated team, bringing innovation and excellence to the catering industry. With a passion for creating new and unique recipes, he ensures that every event is a delightful culinary experience.
